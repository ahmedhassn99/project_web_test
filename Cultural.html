It is one of the departments of the General Administration of Youth Welfare, which is important for providing cultural, literary, scientific and religious benefit to the university students to complement the objectives of the university educational process with its various academic and scientific specializations.




Cultural Activity objectives:


Provide an opportunity for the culture and literature activity of the university youth to practice their hobbies and preferences. Train youth to discuss and confront contemporary issues facing society. Protect youth from the scourge of intellectual and behavioral deviation Revive the Arabic language and protecting it from collapse Achieve the state strategy and the objectives of the National Youth Council in overcoming the obstacles of the reform movements in the country Revive the feelings of loyalty and the spirit of belonging among young people Confront the political negativity, inaction and exercising political rights Discover youth cultural and literary talents, nurturing them, refining their talents and presenting them to senior writers, poets and intellectuals.
